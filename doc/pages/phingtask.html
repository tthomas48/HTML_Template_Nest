The Phing Task

Phing is a php build system. You can easily use it to precompile your .nst files like so:

    <target name="compile">

        <taskdef name="compileNest" classname="HTML.Template.Nest.NestCompileTask" />
        <compileNest>
            <fileset dir="test">
                <include name="**/*.nst" />
            </fileset>
        </compileNest>
    </target>
